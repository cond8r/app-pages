SearchCraft Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-04

1. Information We Collect

SearchCraft does not collect any personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, and no user accounts. The app runs entirely on your device.

2. How the App Works

  • Search queries: When you build a query using the 46 advanced search operators and tap Search, the app opens a system in-app WebView pointing at the search engine URL (e.g. https://www.google.com/search?q=...). The query is sent directly from your device to the search engine you target. We never see, log, or relay your queries.
  • Recipes / favorites / history: Stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. They never leave the device. No cloud sync.
  • Custom recipes you create: Saved locally only. Never transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

The in-app WebView loads pages from third-party search engines and websites you choose to query. These third parties (e.g. Google) have their own privacy policies that apply when their pages load. We are not a party to those policies.

4. Data Stored on Device

The following are stored locally and never leave your device:

  • Search history (you can clear from Settings)
  • Custom recipes you save
  • Built-in recipes you favorite
  • App preferences

Deleting the app removes all this data.

5. Children's Privacy

SearchCraft is rated 17+ because the in-app WebView can load any web content. We do not knowingly collect data from children. We do not collect any data from anyone.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.

7. Contact

Questions or concerns: kernelmd2@163.com

SearchCraft 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-05-04

1. 我们收集什么

SearchCraft 不收集任何个人信息。我们没有服务器、没有分析统计、没有遥测、没有跟踪、没有账户系统。整个 app 全部在你设备本地运行。

2. App 工作方式

  • 搜索查询：你用 46 个高级搜索运算符构造查询点击搜索后，app 在内置 WebView 里打开搜索引擎的 URL（例如 https://www.google.com/search?q=...）。查询从你设备直接发到目标搜索引擎，我们看不到、不记录、不中转。
  • 食谱 / 收藏 / 历史：用 Apple SwiftData 存在你设备本地。从不离开设备。无云同步。
  • 自定义食谱：仅本地保存。从不上传。

3. 第三方服务

内置 WebView 会加载你选择查询的第三方搜索引擎和网站页面。这些第三方（如 Google）有自己的隐私政策，访问其页面时适用其政策。我们不是其政策的一方。

4. 设备本地存储数据

以下数据仅本地存储，从不离开你的设备：

  • 搜索历史（可在设置里清除）
  • 你保存的自定义食谱
  • 你收藏的内置食谱
  • App 偏好

删除 app 即清除所有数据。

5. 儿童隐私

SearchCraft 评级 17+，因为内置 WebView 可加载任意网页内容。我们不会有意收集儿童数据 — 也不收集任何人的数据。

6. 政策变更

如有变更，我们会更新顶部"最后更新"日期。重大变更也会在 app 更新说明中提及。

7. 联系

问题或反馈：kernelmd2@163.com
