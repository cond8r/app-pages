NetTerminal Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-30

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. NetTerminal is a network diagnostic utility. All operations are performed on-device or through direct network connections initiated by the user.

2. Information Used by Features

  • Local network access: When you scan your local network or run service detection, the app discovers devices on the same Wi-Fi. No discovered information is sent to us or any third party.
  • DNS queries: The app queries DNS-over-HTTPS providers (AliDNS, DNSPod, Cloudflare). The domain you query is sent to the DNS provider, which is standard for any DNS resolution.
  • Speed test: Connects to public Cloudflare endpoints. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to those connections.
  • SSH: Connections you initiate go directly to the host you specify. We never proxy or store credentials.
  • Microphone: Used only when you press the voice input button to convert your speech to a command. Audio is processed by Apple Speech Recognition and is not retained by NetTerminal.

3. Data Storage

Command history, saved server profiles, and aliases are stored locally on your device in app-private storage. They never leave your device.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit (in-app purchase)
  • AliDNS, DNSPod, Cloudflare DNS-over-HTTPS
  • Cloudflare speedtest endpoints

5. Children's Privacy

NetTerminal does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.

7. Contact

Questions: kernelmd2@163.com

NetTerminal 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-04-30

1. 我们收集的信息

我们不收集任何个人信息。NetTerminal 是一款网络诊断工具，所有操作均在设备本地执行，或通过用户主动发起的网络连接进行。

2. 各功能使用的信息

  • 本地网络访问：当你扫描局域网或进行服务识别时，App 发现同 Wi-Fi 下的设备。发现的信息不会发送给我们或任何第三方。
  • DNS 查询：App 通过 DNS-over-HTTPS（阿里 AliDNS、腾讯 DNSPod、Cloudflare）解析域名。你查询的域名会发给 DNS 服务商，这是标准 DNS 解析行为。
  • 网速测试：连接 Cloudflare 公共节点，适用 Cloudflare 隐私政策。
  • SSH：连接由你指定，直连远程主机。我们不代理流量、不存储凭据。
  • 麦克风：仅在你按下语音输入按钮时使用，音频由 Apple Speech Recognition 处理，NetTerminal 不留存。

3. 数据存储

命令历史、已保存服务器配置、命令别名等仅存储在设备本地（App 私有存储），永不上传。

4. 第三方服务

  • Apple StoreKit（应用内购买）
  • 阿里 AliDNS、腾讯 DNSPod、Cloudflare DNS-over-HTTPS
  • Cloudflare 速度测试端点

5. 儿童隐私

NetTerminal 不会主动收集任何人（含 13 岁以下儿童）的数据。

6. 政策变更

我们可能更新此政策，「最后更新」日期会反映变更。

7. 联系方式

问题反馈：kernelmd2@163.com
